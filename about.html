About

This website was made for one simple reason: to give you something fun to do when you have a little free time. Whether you're feeling bored, waiting around, or just looking for a quick distraction, you're in the right place. Here you'll find a small collection of simple mini-games and a virtual instrument. Everything is designed to be easy to pick up and play, perfect for those moments when you just want to kill some time.

What can you play?

  • Feeling competitive? Challenge a friend or go up against the computer in a classic game of Tic-Tac-Toe.
  • Want to test your word skills? Try Wordsy and see how good your vocabulary really is.
  • Looking to sharpen your memory? Card Match will put your pattern-spotting skills to the test.
  • More of a music person? Open up the virtual synthesizer and make some noise, no musical talent required.
